About Us.

An ever growing family of youth living in the ways of our Lord, serving His purposes with zeal and dedication.

Mission.

To be ambasadors of Christ in every aspect of living and to share the good news of our risen Saviour in our youthful days.

Vision.

To be a family bound by love and sealed by the blood of Jesus. Living and fullfiling God`s purpose in the peace and comfort of His House.
